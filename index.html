About Me

Builder. Strategist. Translator.

I'm an analytics and data enablement leader with 15+ years driving enterprise-wide data strategy, AI programs, and operational intelligence across highly regulated industries.

I specialize in building analytics functions from the ground up — not inheriting them and maintaining them, but creating the operating models, standards, infrastructure, and culture that make analytics sustainable at scale.

My career has been defined by one consistent pattern: joining organizations where data is underutilized, building the foundation that makes it trustworthy, and then connecting that foundation directly to the decisions that matter most to executives and boards.

I'm now focused on applying that experience in healthcare people analytics — where workforce decisions have a direct line to patient outcomes.
